EBooking

Reservation App

In today's fast-paced world, efficient and hassle-free travel arrangements are paramount. Ebooking emerges as a revolutionary solution designed to streamline the process of reserving bus tickets for inter-urban journeys.

Offering both a web and mobile application, Ebooking simplifies the entire booking experience with its intuitive interface and seamless functionality. Gone are the days of standing in long queues or dealing with last-minute booking woes. Ebooking brings convenience to the forefront, enabling users to effortlessly secure their travel plans at their fingertips.

About

The development journey behind Ebooking was a fusion of cutting-edge technologies. Leveraging the robustness of Node.js for the backend and harnessing the versatility of Flutter for the mobile application, the development process aimed at creating a cohesive, responsive, and user-centric platform.

The integration of these technologies ensured a scalable and efficient system capable of handling a plethora of user interactions and transactions. Additionally, the inclusion of an admin panel fortified the app, providing insightful analytics to enhance the overall service.
