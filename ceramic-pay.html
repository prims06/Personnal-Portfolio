Ceramic Pay

Promotion App

In the realm of enterprise marketing operations, efficiency and seamless transactions are pivotal. Enter this cutting-edge Flutter mobile application, seamlessly coupled with an Angular Dashboard, designed to streamline the process of scanning QR code tickets generated on the dashboard.

This dynamic app serves as a bridge between the product content and the client's purchasing experience. It allows users to scan QR code tickets generated through the dashboard service, enabling clients to effortlessly purchase products, initiate payments, and track their transactions with ease. This innovative solution redefines the purchasing journey, offering a cohesive and user-friendly interface for both businesses and consumers.

About

The development of this app was a meticulous endeavor to blend the versatility of Flutter for the mobile application with the robustness of Angular for the dashboard interface.

The focus remained on creating a unified platform that harmoniously integrates the generation of QR code tickets from the dashboard service with a seamless scanning and payment experience for clients. This amalgamation of technologies ensures a smooth and efficient flow of information, allowing users to effortlessly track their scans, request payments, and facilitate transactions through the app.

Description

This innovative app serves as a versatile tool in the realm of enterprise marketing, enabling clients to seamlessly interact with products and initiate purchases. Utilizing the app, clients can scan QR code tickets associated with specific products, view their scan history, and effortlessly request payments for their scanned items.

The streamlined interface ensures a user-friendly experience, allowing for quick and secure transactions. By bridging the gap between product content and the purchasing process, this app revolutionizes the way clients interact with and acquire products, offering a convenient and efficient solution for both businesses and consumers.
